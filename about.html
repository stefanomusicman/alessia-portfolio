Learn More About Me.

Welcome to my professional portfolio; thank you for taking the time to learn more about me and some of my favorite pieces. I like to say that I’m a Jackie of all trades and a master of one: storytelling. I enjoy the challenge of getting my boots muddy by trying new things, adapting quickly, and grasping concepts properly to share an intended message effectively with others.

Over the past three years, I have worked as a journalist, freelance writer, and documentarian across the country. But my craft began many years before, at the young age of six to be exact, reciting children’s books in our school’s storytelling competitions. I later competed in public speaking at the secondary school level and participated in Business Case Competitions throughout my marketing studies in college. I am a firm believer that the more you learn, the less you know, and the more questions you will have. This open mentality has allowed me to think critically and listen to others objectively.

What sets me apart is my ability to build trustworthy relationships with people in a way that makes them feel comfortable enough to share their stories with confidence. Because really, is there anything better than human connection? On a personal note, my quest to better understand the world has made me a lover of travel, food (both cooking and eating), and anything outdoors. Born in Montreal, biking is my commute of choice, and having lived in the Rocky Mountains, I breathe hiking and skiing. Does this girl relax, you ask? Why yes, I do: through reading and kickboxing!
